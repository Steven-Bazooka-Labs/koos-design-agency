# Phase Context -- Discover

This is the Discover phase -- divergent exploration of the problem space. We are conducting exploratory research with Amsterdam citizens to understand how they experience municipal services across three domains: permits, reporting public space issues, and social services.

Analysis in this phase should be open and divergent, casting a wide net for patterns, unmet needs, and surprises. Do not jump to solutions. Do not filter findings based on what seems "realistic" to implement. Do not let the municipality's existing digital strategy constrain what you notice. Capture everything -- the most valuable insights are often the unexpected ones.

Pay particular attention to differences in experience across digital literacy levels, language backgrounds, and neighborhoods. The municipality's commitment to "no one left behind" means findings about underserved groups carry extra weight.

When in doubt, include rather than exclude. The Define phase will handle prioritization and convergence. This phase is about breadth and depth of understanding.
