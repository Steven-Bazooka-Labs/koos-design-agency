# Phase Context -- Develop

This is the Develop phase -- divergent solution generation and testing. Building on the problem definitions and opportunity areas from Define, this phase explores possible solutions through ideation, concept development, and co-creation sessions with citizens and municipality stakeholders.

Concepts should be evaluated against the needs, jobs, pains, and gains identified in the Discover and Define phases. Prioritize concepts that address the highest-impact opportunities and that work across digital literacy levels. Include citizens in the evaluation -- do not rely solely on internal team judgment.

Document which citizen needs each concept addresses and capture participant reactions during concept testing. Both strong positive and strong negative reactions are valuable data.
