# Project Context -- Amsterdam Municipality

## Client

Municipality of Amsterdam (Gemeente Amsterdam). Amsterdam is home to approximately 850,000 citizens and is one of Europe's most diverse cities, with over 180 nationalities represented. The municipality is committed to inclusive digital services and accessible government for all residents.

## Strategic Challenge

Amsterdam is modernizing its citizen-facing services as part of a broader digital transformation. The central tension: digital transformation must work for all citizens regardless of age, digital literacy, language, or ability. No one gets left behind. The municipality needs to understand how citizens actually experience municipal services today before investing in solutions.

## Key Stakeholders

- **Wethouder (Alderman) for Digitalization** -- Political sponsor. Wants visible progress before next municipal elections.
- **Head of Citizen Services** -- Operational owner. Responsible for service desks, permits, and citizen-facing processes.
- **Digital Team** -- Internal team building and maintaining digital channels. Wants systemic, long-term improvements.
- **Frontline Service Desk Staff** -- Daily contact with citizens. Want their workload reduced and better tools to help people.

## Prior KOOS Work with Amsterdam

KOOS has an established relationship with the Municipality of Amsterdam. Previous engagements include:

- **Inclusive digital services for Amsterdam** -- Service Design Award 2020 Finalist. Focused on making municipal digital services accessible to all citizens.
- **Teacher shortage reduction** -- Service design interventions that helped reduce Amsterdam's teacher shortage from 18.7% to 12.2%.

## Project Structure

This project follows KOOS's four-phase Double Diamond approach:

1. **Discover** (current phase) -- Exploratory research with citizens to understand how they experience municipal services.
2. **Define** -- Synthesize findings into problem statements, personas, journey maps, and opportunity areas.
3. **Develop** -- Generate and test solution concepts through co-creation with citizens and stakeholders.
4. **Deliver** -- Validate solutions through usability testing and pilot evaluations.

## Project Goal

Understand how Amsterdam citizens experience municipal services, identify the biggest improvement opportunities, and build an evidence base that guides the municipality's investment in service improvements. The Discover phase focuses on listening -- casting a wide net for patterns, unmet needs, and surprises before narrowing down.
