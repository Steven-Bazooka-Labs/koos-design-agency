# Phase Context -- Deliver

This is the Deliver phase -- convergent validation and refinement. Selected concepts from the Develop phase are tested through usability testing, pilot evaluations, and stakeholder review.

Focus on what works, what does not, and what needs iteration before launch. Evaluate solutions against the original citizen needs and problem statements from Define. Document usability issues with severity ratings and clear recommendations.

Pay attention to how solutions perform across different user groups -- particularly citizens with low digital literacy, limited Dutch proficiency, and those in underserved neighborhoods. A solution that works well for digitally fluent citizens but fails for others does not meet the project's inclusion commitment.
