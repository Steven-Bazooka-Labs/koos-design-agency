# Phase 2: Define -- VGZ Healthcare

## Double Diamond Context

The Define phase is the first convergent stage of the Double Diamond. After divergent discovery, the goal is to synthesize findings into a clear problem definition. This means clustering insights, identifying patterns, prioritizing opportunity areas, and framing the design challenge in a way that is specific enough to act on but broad enough to allow creative solutions.

## Phase Status

**Completed.**

## What Happened

Discovery findings were synthesized into design principles, opportunity areas, and a prioritized set of patient needs. The team mapped the current patient journey, identified moments of truth and failure points, and defined the core design challenge: how might VGZ give policyholders meaningful control over their health administration without compromising trust, safety, or accessibility?

## Key Outputs

- Synthesized need statements
- Opportunity area prioritization
- Design principles for the portal concept
- Current-state journey map
- Problem framing and "How Might We" statements

## Relevance to Current Phase

Define outputs directly shaped the portal concept and its feature set. During Develop analysis, verify whether the concept being tested aligns with the needs and principles established in Define. If the concept has drifted from defined needs, flag this.
