# Phase 1: Discover -- VGZ Healthcare

## Double Diamond Context

The Discover phase is the first divergent stage of the Double Diamond. The goal is to explore broadly: understand the problem space, gather diverse perspectives, and surface needs and behaviours that may not be immediately obvious. In this phase, the research is generative -- the aim is not to validate solutions but to build deep empathy with the people affected.

## Phase Status

**Completed.**

## What Happened

Exploratory research was conducted with VGZ policyholders and internal stakeholders to understand the current patient experience with health insurance administration. Research activities included contextual interviews with policyholders across different life stages and health situations, shadowing of VGZ call centre agents, and analysis of existing customer feedback data.

## Key Outputs

- Raw interview transcripts and field notes
- Initial thematic observations
- Call centre interaction patterns
- Existing pain point inventory from customer feedback channels

## Relevance to Current Phase

Discover findings informed the Define phase and subsequently shaped the portal concept now being tested in Develop. When analysing Develop phase data, reference Discover findings to assess whether the portal concept addresses the needs originally surfaced.
