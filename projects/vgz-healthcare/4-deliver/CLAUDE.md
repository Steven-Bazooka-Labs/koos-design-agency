# Phase 4: Deliver -- VGZ Healthcare

## Double Diamond Context

The Deliver phase is the second convergent stage of the Double Diamond. After testing and iterating on solutions in Develop, the goal is to converge on a refined, validated solution and prepare it for implementation. This phase focuses on finalising designs, building implementation roadmaps, and handing off to development teams with clear specifications and evidence-backed rationale.

## Phase Status

**Upcoming.** This phase has not yet started. It will begin after Develop concept testing is complete and findings have been synthesized.

## What Will Happen

Based on Develop phase findings, the team will:

1. **Refine the portal concept** -- Incorporate participant feedback, address identified gaps, and adjust or deprioritize features that did not resonate.
2. **Produce a validated design specification** -- A detailed design package grounded in research evidence, ready for VGZ's development team.
3. **Create an implementation roadmap** -- Phased rollout recommendations based on feature readiness, risk, and patient impact.
4. **Deliver stakeholder presentation** -- Present findings and recommendations to VGZ leadership, including evidence for each recommendation.
5. **Define success metrics** -- Refine the preliminary success metrics from the stakeholder workshop based on what concept testing revealed about realistic adoption and impact.

## Relevance to Analysis

If analyses are conducted during this phase, they should focus on:

- Validation of refinements against original participant data
- Ensuring that implementation recommendations do not introduce assumptions that were not supported by research
- Verifying that the final concept addresses the needs of all participant segments, not just the majority
