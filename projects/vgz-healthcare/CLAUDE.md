# VGZ Healthcare -- Project Context

## Client

**Cooperatie VGZ** is the largest health insurer in the Netherlands, serving millions of policyholders through brands including VGZ, Univé, UMC Zorgverzekering, and IZZ. As a cooperative, VGZ operates without shareholders -- its surplus is reinvested into better care and lower premiums for members.

## Strategic Challenge

VGZ is working to transform from a traditional health insurer into a customer-centric digital health partner. Today, digital touchpoints are fragmented and limited: policyholders interact through a basic app for declarations, a separate website for policy management, and phone lines for everything else. There is no unified patient-facing portal that gives policyholders meaningful control over their health journey. The ambition is to close this gap -- but only if the resulting product genuinely serves patient needs rather than just reducing operational cost.

## Key Stakeholders

- **Director of Digital Innovation** -- Sponsors the portal initiative, accountable for digital transformation roadmap
- **Product Owner for Patient Portal** -- Owns the backlog and prioritization for the portal concept
- **Head of Customer Experience** -- Responsible for NPS and cross-channel experience consistency
- **Medical Advisors** -- Clinical experts who evaluate whether self-service features are safe and appropriate for patients

## Prior KOOS Work with VGZ

KOOS has an established relationship with VGZ through several previous engagements:

- **Service redesign** resulting in a 13% increase in Net Promoter Score
- **Pharmacy toolkit** deployed in 400+ pharmacies across the Netherlands (Service Design Award 2020 Finalist)
- **Payment issues reduction** for young adult policyholders through journey redesign
- **Digitising mental health therapy** intake and referral processes

This history provides deep domain knowledge and existing trust with VGZ stakeholders.

## Current Project

This is a four-phase engagement following the Double Diamond framework:

1. **Discover** -- Completed. Exploratory research into patient needs and current pain points.
2. **Define** -- Completed. Synthesized findings into design principles and opportunity areas.
3. **Develop** -- Active. Concept testing a digital self-service portal with VGZ policyholders using a clickable prototype.
4. **Deliver** -- Upcoming. Refinement and handoff of validated concepts for development.

## Project Goal

Validate whether the proposed digital self-service portal meets real patient needs and identify critical gaps before VGZ commits development investment. The portal concept includes claims tracking, medication management, care provider search, and digital referral letter submission. The question is not whether VGZ *can* build this, but whether patients *want* it -- and whether the proposed design serves diverse patient populations including elderly, chronically ill, and digitally less confident policyholders.
