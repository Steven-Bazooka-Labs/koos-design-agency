# Phase Context -- Define

## Phase Purpose

Synthesize Discover phase findings into actionable problem statements, personas, journey maps, and opportunity areas. This phase narrows the wide-angle view from Discover into specific, prioritized directions for the multimodal journey planner.

## Research Approach

Synthesis and framing. No new primary research in this phase. Work from the Discover phase evidence base to define the problem space, cluster passenger needs, and identify the highest-impact opportunity areas for NS.

## Key Activities

- Cluster findings from Discover into thematic areas
- Develop needs-based personas grounded in passenger evidence
- Map current-state customer journeys for key multimodal trip types
- Define problem statements that frame the design challenge
- Prioritize opportunity areas based on passenger impact and strategic alignment with NS's mobility vision

## Analysis Focus

Ensure that every persona, journey map, and problem statement traces back to participant evidence from Discover. Resist the temptation to design solutions at this stage -- the goal is a shared, evidence-based understanding of the problem before moving into concept development.
