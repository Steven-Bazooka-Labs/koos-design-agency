# Phase Context -- Develop

## Phase Purpose

Generate and test improvement concepts for the multimodal journey planner through co-creation with passengers and the NS product team. This phase moves from understanding the problem to exploring solutions.

## Research Approach

Co-creative and iterative. Concept testing sessions with passengers, design sprints with the NS product team, and rapid prototyping of planner improvements. The focus is on generating multiple solution directions and testing them early before committing to implementation.

## Key Activities

- Ideation workshops with NS product, engineering, and CX teams
- Concept development based on Define phase opportunity areas
- Prototype creation for high-priority improvements
- Concept testing sessions with passengers to gauge reactions and preferences
- Iteration based on passenger feedback

## Analysis Focus

Evaluate concept viability from the passenger perspective. Look for strong positive signals (excitement, immediate understanding) as well as concerns or confusion. Pay attention to which concepts resonate with which passenger segments -- a solution that works for commuters may not work for tourists, and vice versa.
